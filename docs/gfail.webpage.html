gfail.webpage.get_alert(HaggLS, HaggLQ, binLS=[100.0, 850.0, 4000.0], binLQ=[70.0, 500.0, 1200.0])[source]¶

Bin edges (3 values) between Green and Yellow, Yellow and Orange, and Orange and Red.

LS based on Nowicki et al. (2014) model results:

  • Red >4000
  • Orange 850–4000
  • Yellow 100–850
  • Green <100

LQ ased on Zhu et al. (2017) general model results:

  • Red >1000
  • Orange 120–1000
  • Yellow 70–120
  • Green <70
Parameters:
  • HaggLS (float) – Aggregate landslide hazard.
  • HaggLQ (float) – Aggregate liquefaction hazard.
  • binLS (list) – List of alert cutoffs for landsliding.
  • binLQ (list) – List of alert cutoffs for liquefaction.
